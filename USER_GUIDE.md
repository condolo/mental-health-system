# User Guide — Embracing The Journey Within v2.1

*Your daily mental wellness companion*

---

## Getting Started

### 1. Create Your Account
When you open the app for the first time, you'll see the **Sign In / Sign Up** screen.
- Tap **Sign Up** to create your account
- Enter your name, email and a password (minimum 6 characters)
- Your account is created instantly — you are taken straight to the app

> 🔒 **Your privacy is guaranteed.** Your journal entries are encrypted to your account only. No one — not even the therapist — can read your personal reflections, moods, or notes.

### 2. Daily Check-In
Every day, tap **"Start Your Check-In"** from the home screen. You'll move through 6 sections:

| # | Section | What you do |
|---|---------|-------------|
| 1 | **Mood Check-In** | Select how you're feeling right now (10 options) |
| 2 | **Gratitude Garden** | Write 3 things you're grateful for |
| 3 | **Affirmations** | Choose "I am…" statements that resonate, or write your own |
| 4 | **Self-Care Check** | Tick habits you completed today (water, sleep, movement, etc.) |
| 5 | **Stress & Coping** | Rate your stress (1–10), identify the source, log how you coped |
| 6 | **My Reflection** | Write a key lesson, your intention for tomorrow, one word for today |

After completing all sections, you'll see a **celebration screen** with a summary of your day.

## Features

### 📊 My Insights
Found in the bottom navigation bar under **Insights**.

**Daily Insight**
- Compares today's mood, stress and self-care to yesterday
- Shows whether things are improving, stable or need attention
- Gives a personalised suggestion (e.g. a coping tip, or a recommendation to book a therapy session)

**Weekly Report**
- Runs after you have at least 2 days of entries
- Shows your average mood, average stress, average self-care score
- Displays your mood trend (📈 Improving / ➡️ Stable / 📉 Declining)
- Includes a 7-day chart of all three metrics
- Generates personalised suggestions based on your patterns

> ⚠️ If your report shows a **red alert**, it means your stress or mood has been consistently challenging. The app will suggest booking a therapy session — please do reach out. You deserve support.

---

### 📅 Events & Sessions
Found in the bottom navigation bar under **Events**.

Here you can see all upcoming events organised by the therapist team, including:
- 🧠 Individual/Group Therapy Sessions
- 💬 Mental Health Talks
- 🛠️ Workshops
- 🤝 Support Groups
- 🧘 Meditation Sessions
- 💻 Webinars

**How to book:**
1. Browse the events list
2. Tap **"Book My Spot →"** on the event you want
3. You'll receive a **Confirmation Code** (e.g. `JTW-A7BC2D3E`)
4. The booking is saved in **My Bookings** (side menu or Events → My Bookings)

**Cancelling:**
Tap "Cancel" on your booked event. Cancellations free up a spot for others.

---

### ⭐ Active Member Status & Discounts
After completing **10 check-ins**, you are automatically promoted to **Active Member** status.

**What you get:**
- A unique personal discount code (e.g. `ACTIVE-A1B2C3`)
- The discount (default 20%) is **automatically applied** when you book a premium session — no manual entry needed
- A notification in your bell when you unlock the status

Your Active Member badge is visible on the Events page.

---

### 🔔 Notifications
The **bell icon** (🔔) in the top-right of the header shows your notifications.

You will receive notifications for:
- New events and sessions added by the therapist
- Your booking confirmations
- Achievement unlocks (e.g. Active Member status)
- System announcements from the therapy team

Tap the bell to open the panel. Tap **"Mark all read"** to clear the badge.

---

### 📖 My Journal (History)
Your complete history of daily check-ins, visible in the **Journal** tab.
Each entry shows: mood, stress level, self-care score, first gratitude, day word and intention.

---

### 🌊 Breathing Exercise
When your stress level is **7 or above**, a 4-7-8 breathing exercise appears on the Stress section:
1. **Inhale** for 4 seconds
2. **Hold** for 7 seconds
3. **Exhale** for 8 seconds
Repeat 3 cycles. This is clinically proven to reduce acute anxiety.

## Privacy & Data

| What is stored | Who can see it |
|----------------|---------------|
| Your name and email | Only you |
| Your mood, stress, reflections, gratitude | **Only you** |
| Whether you completed a check-in (date only) | Anonymous aggregate only — admin sees counts, not content |
| Your booking for an event | You and the therapist (name + email for attendance) |

The therapist/admin dashboard shows **zero private journal data** — by design. It only shows anonymous statistics (e.g. "47 check-ins this week" — never who wrote what).

### 📄 Weekly Report PDF
After completing at least 2 days of check-ins, a **Download Report** button appears on the Insights page.

- Tap it to generate a private, branded PDF of your weekly wellness report
- Includes your mood, stress, self-care averages, 7-day chart and personalised suggestions
- The PDF is generated on your device — it is never sent to any server
- The document is marked **Private & Confidential**
- Save it, print it, or share with a healthcare provider as you choose

### 👩‍⚕️ Your Therapist
Found in the side menu under **Your Therapist**.

See your therapist's public profile including their title, bio, areas of expertise, languages spoken and contact details. From this page you can:
- **Send a Message** — opens the private chat
- **Book a Session** — opens the availability calendar

---

### 💬 Private Chat
Found in the bottom navigation bar and side menu.

Send private messages directly to your therapist. Your therapist will reply during working hours.

**Confidentiality:** All messages are strictly private. No one else — not other users, not third parties — can see your conversation. The red badge on the chat icon shows when you have a new reply.

---

### 🩺 Book a 1-to-1 Session
Found in the side menu under **My Sessions**.

**How to book:**
1. Tap **My Sessions** → **Book a Session** tab
2. Browse available dates and times
3. Choose Online or In-person (where applicable)
4. Tap **Book This Slot →** to confirm
5. You will receive a confirmation code (e.g. `SES-A7BC2D3E`) and a notification
6. For online sessions, a meeting link will be provided

**My Sessions tab** shows all your upcoming and past sessions.

**Cancellation:** Tap **Cancel Session** on any upcoming confirmed session to free the slot.

> 🔒 **Confidentiality notice:** Everything discussed in a session is strictly confidential and will not be disclosed without your explicit consent, except as required by law.

## Tips for Best Results

- ✅ Try to check in **at the same time every day** (morning or evening works well)
- ✅ Be **honest** — no one else will see your entries
- ✅ Even a **partial check-in** is valuable
- ✅ Check your **weekly insights** every Sunday to see your progress
- ✅ Attend events regularly — the community and professional support make a real difference
- ✅ If you notice a **red alert** in your insights, take it seriously — book a session
